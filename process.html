Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our process and methodology

How long does the entire process take?

Our typical project timeline is 18-27 days from start to launch, depending on the complexity of your requirements. This includes all four phases: Discovery (3-5 days), Design (5-7 days), Development (7-10 days), and Testing & Launch (3-5 days).

What information do you need to start the discovery phase?

We'll need details about your business goals, target audience, existing branding (if any), competitors, and specific features you want in your website. We provide a detailed questionnaire to gather all necessary information during our initial consultation.

How involved do I need to be during the process?

We keep you involved at key milestones while handling the heavy lifting. You'll participate in initial discovery sessions, provide feedback during design reviews, and be involved in testing before launch. We aim for a collaborative approach without overwhelming you.

What happens after the website launches?

We provide 30 days of free support post-launch to address any issues and make minor adjustments. After this period, we offer maintenance packages for ongoing updates, security monitoring, and performance optimization.

Can you work with my existing branding and design elements?

Absolutely! We can incorporate your existing branding, colors, and design elements while optimizing them for better conversions. If you don't have existing branding, we can create a complete visual identity as part of the process.
