Global Learning Academy - Detailed Case Study

How we increased online course sales by 45% with strategic optimization

Client Background

Global Learning Academy is a US-based educational company offering professional certification courses in various industries. They were struggling with low conversion rates and couldn't effectively reach international students.

The Challenge

  • Conversion rate below industry average at 1.2%
  • No multi-language support limiting international reach
  • Complex checkout process with high abandonment rate
  • Limited payment options for international students
  • Mobile experience not optimized for course consumption

Our Solution

  • Implemented multi-language LMS supporting 12 languages
  • Optimized checkout flow reducing steps from 5 to 2
  • Integrated global payment gateways (Stripe, PayPal, local options)
  • Mobile-first design with offline course access
  • Progress tracking and certification automation
  • Email automation for course completion and renewals

Technical Implementation

We built a custom WordPress/LearnDash solution with React components for the interactive elements. The platform features:

  • Custom user dashboard with progress tracking
  • Automated certificate generation upon course completion
  • Multi-currency pricing with region-specific discounts
  • Video hosting with adaptive bitrate streaming
  • Quiz and assessment system with instant feedback
